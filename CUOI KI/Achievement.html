CATEGORY: THE DEMON KING

Faker - The conqueror of titles

Lee Sang-hyeok, popularly known by his in-game name Faker, is a professional League of Legends player from South Korea. Widely regarded as the greatest esports player of all time, Faker's career is marked by numerous achievements and unforgettable moments that have solidified his legendary status in the world of competitive gaming.

The achievements collector

Faker's list of accomplishments is unparalleled in the esports community. Notable Events: 2013 World Championship Victory: Faker burst onto the international stage with his team, SK Telecom T1, by winning the 2013 World Championship. His iconic play against KT Rolster Bullets, where he outplayed Ryu in a Zed versus Zed mirror match, remains one of the most memorable moments in esports history. 2015 and 2016 World Championships: Faker led SKT to back-to-back World Championships in 2015 and 2016, showcasing his dominance and adaptability in an ever-evolving meta. His performances during these tournaments cemented his status as the best player in the world. 2017 MSI and Rift Rivals: Faker's victories at the 2017 Mid-Season Invitational and Rift Rivals highlighted his ability to adapt to different international playstyles and maintain his dominance on a global scale. 2023 and 2024 World Championships: Faker's resurgence in the later years of his career, winning the 2023 and 2024 World Championships, proved that he still had the skill and determination to compete at the highest level. These victories were particularly significant, demonstrating his longevity and enduring impact on the game. Asian Games 2022: Faker represented South Korea at the Asian Games, winning a gold medal in esports. This achievement not only added to his illustrious career but also showcased the growing recognition of esports as a legitimate competitive field.
